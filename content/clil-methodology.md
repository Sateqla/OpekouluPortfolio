# CLIL Methodology

Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL) is an educational approach where a foreign language is used for the learning and teaching of both content and language.

### Benefits
- **Naturalistic Environment**: Provides a context for language acquisition.
- **Dual Focus**: Teaches subjects like History or Science while improving language skills.
- **Cultural Awareness**: Often involves exposure to different cultures.
